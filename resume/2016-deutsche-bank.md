<a name="deutsche-bank"></a>
## **Client**: Deutsche Bank  
**Location**: Frankfurt, Germany  
**Duration**: 2016  

---

### **Objective**:  
Empower Product Owners and team members from various vendors by teaching the fundamentals of **Scrum**, introducing **User Story Mapping**, and incorporating **Kanban elements** to enhance collaboration and efficiency within the organization.

---

### **Solution**:  

- **Scrum Training**:  
   - Provided comprehensive training to **Product Owners** and team members on the basics of **Scrum**, ensuring a solid understanding of roles, ceremonies, and artifacts.  
   - Focused on fostering an agile mindset and aligning teams with Scrum principles.

- **User Story Mapping Introduction**:  
   - Introduced the technique of **User Story Mapping** to help teams visualize the product backlog and prioritize features based on user needs and business value.  
   - Conducted interactive workshops to practice creating user story maps.

- **Three-Day Learning Week**:  
   - Organized an immersive **three-day learning week** to delve deeper into agile practices.  
   - Introduced **Kanban elements** to complement Scrum practices, emphasizing workflow visualization and continuous improvement.  
   - Taught the concept of **"3 Amigos"** (collaboration between Business Analyst, Developer, and Tester) using paper prototypes to enhance communication and shared understanding.

- **Consultation on Team Structure and Agile Adoption**:  
   - Advised management on optimal **team size and composition** to maximize efficiency and collaboration.  
   - Consulted on how **Scrum** and agile methodologies could be effectively integrated into the organization's existing processes.  
   - Provided recommendations for scaling agile practices across multiple teams and vendors.

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Successfully equipped Product Owners and team members with the knowledge and skills to implement **Scrum** effectively.  
- Enhanced collaboration and communication among cross-functional teams through **User Story Mapping** and the **3 Amigos** approach.  
- Introduced **Kanban elements** that allowed teams to visualize workflows and identify bottlenecks, leading to process improvements.  
- Provided strategic insights on team structure and agile adoption, assisting Deutsche Bank in leveraging Scrum for organizational benefits.

---

### **Key Contributions**:  
- Delivered tailored training sessions on **Scrum** fundamentals to diverse teams.  
- Facilitated interactive workshops on **User Story Mapping** and **Kanban practices**.  
- Introduced innovative learning methods, such as paper prototypes, to teach the **3 Amigos** concept.  
- Consulted with management on aligning Scrum practices with organizational goals and team dynamics.
