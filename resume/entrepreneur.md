# **Entrepreneurial Case Studies: Building Companies and Solutions**

## **Caimito Services LLC**  
**Founder & Managing Director**  
**Duration**: 2024 - present | **Location**: USA  

### **Objective**:  
Develop an **agroforestry planning and design tool** to support sustainable farming and land restoration initiatives.

---

### **Key Contributions**:  
- Leveraged **ChatGPT** extensively for researching plant species properties and speeding up development tasks.  
- Used **GitHub Copilot** in **VS Code** to quickly prototype the tool's architecture.  
- Implemented a **trunk-based development** workflow to enable seamless delivery of features directly into the production environment.  
- Built the backend with **Spring Boot** and designed a lightweight, **htmx-powered HTML5/CSS3 frontend** for interactivity without heavy frameworks.  

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Successfully delivered a functional planning and design tool to assist in agroforestry projects, combining research with practical development tools.

## **Caimito Agile Life**  
**Founder & Managing Director**  
**Duration**: 2021 - present | **Location**: Andalusia, Spain  

### **Objective**:  
Promote an **ecosystem restoration project** in Andalusia by developing an innovative e-commerce and ERP solution to support product sales and farm management.  

---

### **Key Contributions**:  
- **CaimitoEU** (Frontend Application):  
   - Developed a Vue.js-based frontend application to facilitate:  
      - Sales of **packaged products** and **Iberico meat**.  
      - Multi-language support (English, German, Spanish) with **vue-i18n**.  
   - Integrated **PayPal** for seamless payment processing.  
   - Secured communication via **Keycloak** (OAuth2) to interact with backend APIs.  
   - Built a CI/CD pipeline with **GitHub Actions**, ensuring reliable deployments with feature flags for complex features.  

- **GranjaEU** (Backend ERP System):  
   - Created a small-scale ERP for farm operations using **Spring Boot**, **MongoDB**, and **PostgreSQL**.  
   - Implemented RESTful APIs to support the frontend application and manage farm operations.  
   - Automated email notifications with **JavaMail** and Google SMTP.  
   - Ensured robust testing with **JUnit** and **Cypress** for both backend and frontend systems.  

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Successfully delivered an integrated e-commerce and ERP platform to manage farm operations and promote sustainable ecosystem restoration.  
- Achieved frequent, reliable deployments through trunk-based development and CI/CD pipelines.
