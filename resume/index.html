Résumé Stephan Schwab

I am a person with a wide range of T-shaped skills and experiences in many different domains.

Author

My book Smarter Software with Activity-Centered Design teaches readers how to discover what users want to do through and thus helps them to create a solution for the right problem.

I write many blog posts on my personal blog here in English, German, and sometimes in Spanish. On my company's website I provide all the content in English and German. Further, I also maintain a blog about Activity-Centered Design.

Coach

I coach software development teams and their management on the development of organisational and technical capabilities.

Consultant

For clients I've provided advice in the domains of network security, network design, software development and quality management.

Designer

I do my own web design for this site and also my company's website.

In the domain of physical things I have designed my own custom-built offroad trailer and also a temporary horse stable.

Developer

Over time I've used about 20 different programming languages (functional, procedural, object-oriented, dynamic) on multiple platforms. I've worked deep down in the system close to the hardware and created end-user software with various high-level frameworks.

Entrepreneur

I founded and managed companies in software development and telecommunication in Germany, USA, and Panama.

While living in Panama I ventured out into forestry services, farming, land development, and residential construction.

Pilot

In 1989 I earned my Private Pilot License (PPL) for single engine piston aircraft. After not using it for about 17 years I reactivated it in 2013 for doing business trips, and sometimes for leisure, throughout Europe in a light aircraft.

Speaker

So far I have presented and spoken in English and Spanish for audiences at public and private events. My topics have been from the domains of Software Development and Management.

Trainer

Frequently I teach on topics in software development at the level of advanced and higher.
