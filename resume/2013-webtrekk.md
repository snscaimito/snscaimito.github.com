## **Client**: Webtrekk  
**Location**: Berlin, Germany  
**Duration**: 2013 - 2014  

---

### **Objective**:  
Support Webtrekk's transition to agile practices by coaching leadership and Scrum Masters, and introducing **Acceptance Test-Driven Development (ATDD)** to improve collaboration and product quality.

---

### **Solution**:  

- **Coaching the CTO**:  
   - Provided tailored coaching to the **CTO** on agile principles, emphasizing iterative development, team autonomy, and continuous delivery.  
   - Guided the adoption of **testing strategies** to ensure product quality and alignment with agile processes.  

- **Introduction of ATDD**:  
   - Introduced **Acceptance Test-Driven Development (ATDD)** as a collaborative method to align developers, testers, and stakeholders.  
   - Delivered practical workshops using tools like:  
      - **Cucumber** for writing human-readable, executable acceptance tests.  
      - **Ruby** for scripting and test automation.  

- **Scrum Master Coaching**:  
   - Paired with resident **Scrum Masters** to coach them on facilitating agile ceremonies and supporting team success.  
   - Helped Scrum Masters build strong foundations for fostering agile collaboration and continuous improvement within their teams.

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Equipped the **CTO** with the knowledge and strategies to lead agile transformations and support testing improvements.  
- Enabled teams to adopt **ATDD**, improving collaboration and ensuring product requirements were well-tested and validated.  
- Enhanced the effectiveness of **Scrum Masters** through hands-on coaching, helping them better serve their teams and drive agile adoption.

---

### **Technologies Used**:  
- **Languages**: Java, Ruby  
- **Testing Framework**: Cucumber  

---

### **Key Contributions**:  
- Coached the **CTO** on agile principles and testing practices to align leadership with agile goals.  
- Successfully introduced **ATDD** using Cucumber and Ruby to improve requirement validation and test automation.  
- Paired with and coached **Scrum Masters** to enhance their facilitation and agile leadership capabilities.
