## **Client**: Raiffeisen Bank  
**Locations**: Sarajevo (Bosnia-Herzegovina), Belgrade (Serbia), Pristina (Kosovo)  
**Duration**: 2019  

---

### **Objective**:  
Conduct comprehensive **assessments of IT processes and systems** for the executive boards of Raiffeisen Bank subsidiaries, with plans to establish a new development center.

---

### **Solution**:  
- Performed detailed **on-site assessments** of IT processes, infrastructure, and development systems in three key locations: Sarajevo, Belgrade, and Pristina.  
- Delivered assessment reports with clear recommendations for improving IT systems and workflows.  
- Advised executive boards on opportunities to enhance operational efficiency and align IT processes with strategic business goals.  
- Prepared to transition into an **embedded Developer Advocate role** to help establish and lead a new development center.  
- The engagement was cut short due to the onset of the **COVID-19 pandemic**.

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Provided actionable insights and recommendations to the executive boards for improving IT processes and systems.  
- Laid the groundwork for the creation of a **new development center** to drive innovation and local talent development.  
- Built trust and alignment with stakeholders across multiple subsidiaries before the engagement was interrupted.

---

### **Key Contributions**:  
- Conducted on-site IT process and systems assessments at **three subsidiary locations**.  
- Presented findings and recommendations directly to executive boards.  
- Planned to transition into a leadership role as an embedded **Developer Advocate** for the creation of a development center.  

---

### **Challenges and Adaptation**:  
- The planned long-term engagement was stopped due to the **COVID-19 pandemic**, requiring adaptation and future planning.
