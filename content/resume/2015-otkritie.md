## **Client**: Bank Otkritie  
**Location**: Moscow, Russia  
**Duration**: 2015  

---

### **Objective**:  
Facilitate the formation of effective development teams, provide coaching to management, and create a **Coaching Map** as a guiding tool for future organizational improvements.

---

### **Solution**:  

- **Team Formation Workshops**:  
   - Conducted **initial workshops** focused on team formation and collaboration strategies.  
   - Helped management and team members understand the principles of self-organizing teams and effective communication.

- **Team Coaching for Management**:  
   - Delivered **team coaching sessions** with management to align leadership practices with agile principles.  
   - Focused on building trust, fostering team autonomy, and improving leadership support for teams.

- **Organizational Anamnesis**:  
   - Performed a comprehensive **organizational anamnesis** to analyze the current state of teams, workflows, and management structures.  
   - Identified key challenges and opportunities for improvement in team dynamics and organizational processes.

- **Coaching Map Development**:  
   - Created the **first version of a Coaching Map**, outlining tailored solutions and strategies to guide future team coaching efforts.  
   - Provided actionable recommendations for management to support teams in achieving agility and productivity.

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Successfully initiated team formation through focused workshops and management coaching.  
- Delivered a comprehensive **Coaching Map** to serve as a strategic tool for guiding organizational improvement.  
- Provided leadership with insights and solutions for fostering a culture of collaboration, team autonomy, and continuous improvement.

---

### **Key Contributions**:  
- Facilitated workshops on **team formation** to establish strong foundations for collaboration.  
- Coached management to align their practices with agile team dynamics.  
- Conducted an **organizational anamnesis** to identify challenges and improvement areas.  
- Developed a **Coaching Map** as a practical, strategic tool to guide ongoing coaching and transformation efforts.
