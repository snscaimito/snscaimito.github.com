## **Client**: Virtual Solution  
**Location**: Munich, Germany  
**Duration**: 2015  

---

### **Objective**:  
Enhance team capabilities in **test automation**, **clean code**, and **architecture** while improving **intra-team collaboration** and introducing **Specification by Example** for iOS and Android platforms.

---

### **Solution**:  

- **Workshops in Code Retreat Format**:  
   - Conducted immersive workshops, structured as **code retreats**, to focus on:  
      - **Test automation** strategies for mobile platforms.  
      - Writing **clean code** and improving architectural design.  
      - Enhancing **collaboration** between team members to foster effective teamwork.  
      - Introducing **Specification by Example** to align testing and development through shared understanding.

- **Platform-Specific Focus**:  
   - Tailored sessions for development on both **iOS** (Objective-C) and **Android** (Java).  
   - Demonstrated tools and frameworks for mobile testing and automation, including:  
      - **Appium** for cross-platform mobile test automation.  
      - **Cucumber** for behavior-driven development and executable specifications.

- **Hands-On Exercises**:  
   - Facilitated practical exercises to allow participants to apply clean coding practices, test automation techniques, and Specification by Example in real-world scenarios.  
   - Encouraged knowledge sharing and teamwork through pair programming and group discussions.

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Improved team proficiency in **test automation** using Appium and Cucumber.  
- Enabled teams to write **cleaner, maintainable code** for iOS and Android applications.  
- Fostered better **intra-team collaboration** through hands-on exercises and team-building practices.  
- Equipped teams with a solid understanding of **Specification by Example** to align requirements and automated tests effectively.

---

### **Technologies Used**:  
- **Languages**: Java, Objective-C  
- **Platforms**: Android, iOS  
- **Testing Tools**: Appium, Cucumber  

---

### **Key Contributions**:  
- Designed and delivered workshops using the **code retreat format** to enhance technical and collaborative skills.  
- Introduced **Specification by Example** as a method to align testing and development.  
- Taught test automation techniques with **Appium** and behavior-driven development with **Cucumber**.  
- Improved team collaboration and clean coding practices through hands-on sessions.
