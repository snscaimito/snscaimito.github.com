## **Client**: Huawei Technologies  
**Locations**: Munich, Germany (2015) and Several Cities, China (2014)  
**Duration**: 2014 - 2015  

---

### **Objective**:  
Support Huawei Technologies in managing complexity in software product development by introducing **agile practices** such as **Acceptance Test-Driven Development (ATDD)**, **Situational Kanban**, and coaching middle management on agile principles.

---

### **Solution**:  

#### **2014 - Agile Coaching and ATDD Implementation**  
- **ATDD Introduction**:  
   - Introduced **Acceptance Test-Driven Development (ATDD)** after a previous failed attempt, ensuring teams understood the process of writing tests collaboratively before development.  
   - Delivered hands-on coaching to development teams, teaching them to implement ATDD effectively using tools such as:  
      - **Cucumber** and **Behave** for writing executable acceptance tests.  
      - **Appium** for automating tests on **Android** applications.  
   - Demonstrated the value of ATDD in improving communication between developers, testers, and business stakeholders.

- **Situational Kanban**:  
   - Implemented **Situational Kanban** to help teams manage workflows, identify bottlenecks, and adapt to changing priorities.  
   - Tailored Kanban practices to suit the teamâ€™s unique challenges and context.  

- **Middle Management Coaching**:  
   - Worked closely with **project managers** and **department leaders** to teach **agile principles** and best practices.  
   - Focused on fostering a mindset shift to support team autonomy, iterative delivery, and value-driven development.

---

#### **2015 - Management Report on Complexity**  
- Conducted an analysis of **organizational and human factors** contributing to complexity in software product development.  
- Produced a detailed **management report** outlining:  
   - Key challenges faced in managing complexity.  
   - Recommendations for improving organizational processes, team structures, and leadership approaches.  
   - Strategies for applying agile practices to mitigate complexity and improve product development outcomes.

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Successfully introduced **ATDD** and **Situational Kanban** practices to multiple teams, leading to improved testing processes and workflow management.  
- Coached middle management to adopt **agile principles**, enhancing their ability to support agile teams effectively.  
- Delivered a comprehensive **management report** providing actionable insights for addressing complexity in software product development.  
- Enabled teams to align better on goals, improve collaboration, and deliver higher-quality software.
