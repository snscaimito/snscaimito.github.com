## **Client**: OBI Smart Technologies  
**Location**: Wermelskirchen, Germany  
**Duration**: 2013 - 2014  

---

### **Objective**:  
Lead a development team as an **Interim Scrum Master**, improve team processes, and coach **department heads** on agile values and principles to foster organizational agility.

---

### **Solution**:

- **Scrum Master Leadership**:  
   - Served as the **Interim Scrum Master** for a development group, facilitating agile ceremonies (Daily Standups, Sprint Planning, Retrospectives) and ensuring smooth sprint execution.  
   - Acted as a servant leader to remove blockers, improve team focus, and foster self-organization.

- **Improved Team Structure**:  
   - Added **testers** to the development team to ensure product quality and align testing practices with agile workflows.  
   - Supported the integration of testers into the team to improve collaboration and feedback loops.

- **Requirements Gathering**:  
   - Improved the **requirements gathering process** by introducing structured methods to refine user stories and align them with team priorities.  
   - Enhanced communication with stakeholders to clarify expectations and reduce misunderstandings.

- **Coaching Department Heads**:  
   - Provided coaching to **department heads** on agile values and principles, focusing on:  
      - Iterative development and delivery.  
      - Collaboration and transparency between departments.  
      - Fostering a culture of continuous improvement and team empowerment.

- **Improved Collaboration**:  
   - Strengthened **intra-departmental collaboration** by breaking silos and encouraging shared ownership of deliverables.  
   - Introduced practices to improve alignment between development teams and other departments.

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Led a development group effectively as an **Interim Scrum Master**, enabling improved sprint execution and team cohesion.  
- Enhanced product quality by integrating **testers** into the development workflow.  
- Improved the **requirements gathering process**, leading to better-defined user stories and reduced rework.  
- Fostered organizational agility by coaching department heads on agile principles, resulting in improved collaboration and leadership alignment.  

---

### **Key Contributions**:  
- Led a Scrum team as an **Interim Scrum Master**, improving team performance and delivery.  
- Added testers to the team to align testing with agile workflows and improve quality.  
- Enhanced **requirements gathering** processes for better clarity and focus.  
- Coached **department heads** on agile values and principles to drive organizational agility.  
- Strengthened intra-departmental collaboration and communication.
