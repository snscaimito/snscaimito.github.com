## **Client**: Caimito Development  
**Location**: Panama City, Panama  
**Duration**: 2007 - 2009  

---

### **Objective**:  
Design and develop an **agile lifecycle management tool** to support **distributed Scrum teams** in managing their workflows effectively.

---

### **Solution**:

- **Product Design and Development**:  
   - Conceptualized and developed **Caimito One Team**, a tool specifically designed to address the needs of **distributed Scrum teams**.  
   - Designed features to support:  
      - Sprint and backlog management.  
      - Team collaboration across geographically distributed locations.  
      - Tracking user stories, tasks, and sprint progress.  

- **Focus on Agile Principles**:  
   - Ensured the tool adhered to **Scrum and agile values**, enabling teams to self-organize, visualize their work, and continuously improve.  
   - Integrated user feedback loops to refine the product iteratively.  

- **Technical Implementation**:  
   - Developed the tool with a focus on simplicity, usability, and scalability to meet the needs of teams operating in different time zones.  
   - Provided core functionality for managing agile lifecycles, including task boards, sprint planning, and progress tracking.

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Delivered **Caimito One Team**, a dedicated agile lifecycle management tool that improved the productivity and collaboration of **distributed Scrum teams**.  
- Enabled teams to manage their agile workflows efficiently, regardless of geographic location.  
- Demonstrated the value of purpose-built tools for supporting agile methodologies in distributed environments.  

---

### **Key Contributions**:  
- Designed and developed **Caimito One Team** from concept to deployment.  
- Focused on features that support **distributed Scrum teams**, including sprint management and task tracking.  
- Ensured the product embodied **Scrum principles** to foster team collaboration and transparency.
