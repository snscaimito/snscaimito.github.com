## **Client**: Alfa Insurance  
**Location**: Moscow, Russia  
**Duration**: 2017 - 2018  

---

### **Objective**:  
Uplift engineering practices, improve software architecture, and establish a DevOps structure to enhance collaboration and productivity across development and operations teams.

---

### **Solution**:  

#### **2017 - Initial Assessment and Coaching**  
- Conducted a comprehensive **audit** of the software development and operations groups to identify areas for improvement in processes, skills, and team structures.  
- Delivered findings and recommendations to prepare for a **larger coaching and transformation engagement**.  
- Laid the groundwork for future coaching by providing **initial coaching** to two development teams, focusing on foundational XP (eXtreme Programming) practices.

#### **2018 - Coaching and Engineering Improvements**  
- **Team Coaching**:  
   - Worked directly with multiple teams and senior management to uplift engineering practices, focusing on XP methodologies.  
   - Introduced **XP-style testing practices** by involving a former coachee to support test-driven development at the team level.  
- **Security Consulting**:  
   - Advised on best practices for **network security** and **information security**, ensuring teams aligned with organizational goals.  
- **Architecture Consulting**:  
   - Consulted on transitioning to **Self-Contained Systems (SCS)** architecture using **Docker containers** to improve modularity and deployment flexibility.  
- **Technology Integration**:  
   - Supported teams in leveraging technologies such as **Java, JavaScript, Vaadin**, and **SQL** for application development and delivery.

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Conducted a detailed **audit** that enabled executive leadership to plan a long-term DevOps transformation.  
- Teams adopted improved engineering practices with a focus on **XP methodologies** and testing, resulting in better collaboration and code quality.  
- Successfully introduced architectural improvements through **Self-Contained Systems (SCS)** using Docker containers, enhancing scalability and maintainability.  
- Strengthened team alignment with **network and information security** standards.
