## **Client**: Cloud9 Analytics  
**Location**: San Mateo, California, USA  
**Duration**: 2007  

---

### **Objective**:  
Lead the development of **Cloud9 Messenger**, a web application designed to enhance sales forecasting and pipeline management by delivering key sales reports directly to sales managers' mobile devices.

---

### **Solution**:

- **Role and Collaboration**:  
   - Served as **Software Developer** and **Senior Developer Advocate / Scrum Coach**, collaborating closely with the **Project Manager** and **CEO**.  
   - Employed **Scrum methodology** to manage the project from inception through deployment, ensuring iterative development and continuous feedback.  

- **Development and Implementation**:  
   - Designed and developed the **Cloud9 Messenger** web application, focusing on user-friendly interfaces and seamless integration with existing CRM systems.  
   - Implemented features to automatically generate weekly "What's Changed?" reports, detailing changes in forecast and pipeline, and calculating percent pipeline coverage for each team member.  
   - Ensured compatibility with mobile devices to provide sales managers with real-time access to critical sales data.  

- **Technologies Used**:  
   - **Languages**: Java, Ruby  
   - **Frameworks**: Spring Framework  
   - **Testing Tools**: Cucumber  
   - **Application Server**: IBM WebSphere  
   - **Database**: SQL  

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Successfully launched **Cloud9 Messenger**, enabling sales managers to receive timely and actionable sales insights on their mobile devices.  
- Improved sales forecasting accuracy and pipeline visibility, contributing to more informed decision-making and enhanced sales performance.  
- Demonstrated the effectiveness of **Scrum methodology** in delivering a high-quality product within a collaborative environment.
