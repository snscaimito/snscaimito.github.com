## **Client**: Alfa Bank  
**Location**: Moscow, Russia  
**Duration**: 2016 - 2017  

---

### **Objective**:  
Transform the organization by creating a **DevOps culture** within Alfa Bank’s development divisions, including a comprehensive restructuring to improve collaboration, delivery, and operational efficiency.

---

### **Solution**:

#### **2016 - Initial Assessment and Proposal**  
- Conducted an **anamesis** (in-depth assessment) with multiple development groups over the course of one week.  
- Analyzed team workflows, processes, and bottlenecks to identify opportunities for improvement.  
- Created a detailed **proposal** for management, outlining:  
   - Steps to introduce a **DevOps culture**.  
   - Recommendations for organizational restructuring to foster collaboration between development and operations teams.  
- Presented findings and gained **approval** from leadership to proceed with the transformation plan.

---

#### **2017 - DevOps Implementation and Organizational Restructuring**  
- Worked with management and development teams to implement the approved plan for establishing a **DevOps culture**.  
- Collaborated with leadership to guide the **restructuring of the organization**, aligning roles, teams, and workflows to DevOps principles.  
- Focused on breaking down silos between development and operations to improve communication, delivery pipelines, and release cycles.  
- Facilitated cultural change by introducing best practices, coaching teams, and aligning processes with DevOps methodologies.  

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Successfully initiated the creation of a **DevOps culture** within Alfa Bank’s development division.  
- Gained leadership buy-in for organizational restructuring to align with DevOps principles.  
- Laid the groundwork for long-term improvements in delivery efficiency, collaboration, and team autonomy.

---

### **Key Contributions**:  
- Conducted a comprehensive assessment of development groups and presented an actionable proposal to management.  
- Led the initial implementation of **DevOps culture** and practices.  
- Collaborated on the restructuring of teams to align with DevOps workflows.  
- Established a foundation for sustainable cultural and process improvements.
