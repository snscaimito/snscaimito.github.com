## **Client**: Nationwide Insurance  
**Location**: Columbus, Ohio, USA  
**Duration**: 2008  

---

### **Objective**:  
Coach **software architects** and **development teams** on adopting **agile development practices**, including **Test-Driven Development (TDD)** and **Continuous Integration (CI)**, to improve software quality and delivery efficiency.

---

### **Solution**:

- **Agile Development Coaching**:  
   - Delivered targeted coaching sessions to **software architects** and team members, focusing on:  
      - **Test-Driven Development (TDD)** to improve code quality, maintainability, and early defect detection.  
      - Implementing **Continuous Integration (CI)** pipelines to automate builds, tests, and deployments.  
   - Demonstrated agile best practices to integrate testing into the development process seamlessly.

- **Hands-On Training with Tools and Technologies**:  
   - Conducted practical sessions using:  
      - **Cucumber** for acceptance testing.  
      - **Spring Framework** for backend development.  
      - **IBM WebSphere** for application server deployment.  
      - **SQL** for database integration.  
   - Enabled participants to apply TDD concepts using **Java** and **Ruby**, showcasing the benefits of fast feedback loops and iterative development.

- **Architectural Guidance**:  
   - Provided architectural recommendations to align development processes with agile principles.  
   - Ensured software architects could lead teams effectively while maintaining technical excellence.

---

### **Outcome**:  
- Successfully coached **software architects** and development teams on agile development practices, improving code quality and team efficiency.  
- Enabled the adoption of **TDD** and **Continuous Integration** as core practices, reducing defects and accelerating delivery cycles.  
- Strengthened the teams' ability to integrate testing into development workflows using tools like **Cucumber** and **Spring Framework**.  

---

### **Technologies Used**:  
- **Languages**: Java, Ruby  
- **Testing Tools**: Cucumber  
- **Frameworks**: Spring Framework  
- **Application Server**: IBM WebSphere  
- **Database**: SQL  

---

### **Key Contributions**:  
- Coached **software architects** on leading teams with agile development practices.  
- Taught **Test-Driven Development (TDD)** and **Continuous Integration (CI)** to improve software quality.  
- Delivered hands-on training with tools like **Cucumber**, **Spring Framework**, and **IBM WebSphere**.  
- Enabled development teams to integrate automated testing and CI into their workflows.
